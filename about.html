About Us

Our mission is simple — to make Telegram more fun, friendly, and interactive by providing a bot that feels alive, expressive, and very easy to use.

Baka provides AI responses, group games, economy features, ranks, moderation tools, stickers, media utilities, and many more features—all in one bot.

Baka is designed to make your Telegram experience smoother, safer, and 10× more enjoyable.
