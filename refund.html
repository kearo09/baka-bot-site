Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

The Baka Bot provides digital services, premium memberships, and in-bot features. Since everything is delivered instantly online, we follow a fair and transparent policy for cancellations and refunds.

1. Digital Product Nature

All items and memberships provided by Baka Bot are digital. Once a feature is delivered or activated inside the bot, it is considered **used**.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds will only be provided under the following conditions:

• Payment was made, but premium features were **not activated** due to technical issues.
• Duplicate payments for the same feature.
• Wrong amount charged due to a system error.

Refunds will not be provided if:

• The user has already used or accessed the premium features.
• The purchase was made by mistake but features were delivered.
• User expected a different type of feature or result after using the bot.
• User violated the bot rules and premium was disabled due to abuse.

3. Cancellation Policy

Since Baka Bot delivers all digital features instantly, cancellations are only possible **before the feature is activated**.

4. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be credited back within **5–10 business days** depending on the user’s bank/payment gateway.

5. Extensions or Access Issues

If your premium service expires early or activation fails, contact support and we will restore or extend your access free of cost.

6. Non-Refundable Cases

• Refunds will not be issued for non-usage of service after activation.
• No refunds if the user is banned for breaking Telegram or bot rules.
• No refunds for "change of mind" after feature delivery.
