Refund & Cancellation Workflow

Last Updated: January 2025

Follow these steps to understand eligibility, process, and rules for refunds and cancellations of Baka Bot digital services.

Step 1: Understand Digital Product Nature

All items and memberships are digital. Once a feature is activated, it is considered used.

Step 2: Check Refund Eligibility

Refunds are possible only if:

  • Payment made but automation features not activated due to technical issues.
  • Duplicate payments for the same feature.
  • Wrong amount charged due to system error.

Refunds won't be provided if:

  • Features have already been used.
  • Purchase was made by mistake after delivery.
  • User expected different results.
  • Activation was disabled due to rule violation.
Step 3: Cancellation Policy

Cancellations are only possible before feature activation due to instant digital delivery.

Step 4: Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are credited back within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or payment gateway.

Step 5: Handling Access Issues

If your service expires early or activation fails, contact support to restore or extend access free of cost.

Step 6: Non-Refundable Cases
  • After providing digital service.
  • Change of mind after feature delivery.
Step 7: Contact for Refunds

Submit your request via the Contact Us page and include payment details for verification.
