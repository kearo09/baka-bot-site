Baka Bot – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Baka Bot (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when users interact with our Telegram bot and related services.

By using our bot, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or sensitive data such as phone numbers, emails, contacts, or device information.

We only collect the following:

  • Telegram User ID
  • Username
  • Name (as shown on Telegram)
  • Group IDs (for group-based features)

Bot activity data stored:

  • Economy balance
  • Kills, rob actions, game results
  • Premium status
  • Message count for earnings
  • Items, inventories, puzzle progress

We never store: phone numbers, emails, passwords, media, or contact lists.

2. Why We Collect This Data

  • To run economy features
  • To manage game systems
  • To show rankings like toprich / topkill
  • To provide premium features
  • To track user progress
  • To maintain anti-abuse protections

3. Payments & Premium

If you purchase Premium, payments are processed via secure third-party gateways (such as Razorpay or Telegram Stars).

We do not store:

  • Card/UPI details
  • Payment passwords
  • Billing information

We only store:

  • Transaction success/failure
  • Premium expiry date
  • User ID

4. Log Data

We collect non-personal logs such as command usage and error logs to improve performance. No IP addresses or device info are collected.

5. Cookies

The Telegram bot does not use cookies. Payment gateways may use cookies as per their own policies.

6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third parties only for:

  • Payment processing
  • Basic analytics

7. Children’s Privacy

Baka Bot is intended for users 13 years and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Data Security

We use encryption, secure APIs, and restricted access to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

9. Data Transfer

Your data may be processed on servers located inside or outside India depending on hosting providers.

10. External Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services linked through the bot.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy anytime. Updates take effect immediately after posting.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us. Contact details are provided in this website.
